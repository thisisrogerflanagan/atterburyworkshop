The Shop

I'm available for limited logo design projects. Just the logo. Limited back-and-forth. Reasonable price. With a particular focus on elevating small businesses that can't or don't want to hire a full-blown agency. Let's keep this simple.

By streamlining the process and being very upfront about expectations, I’m able to deliver you a logo quickly—at an afforable price. See more of my latest work.

Here's How it Works

1. Tell me about your brand

I'll gather info on your brand, goals, visusal vibe, competition, etc.

2. I'll sketch some ideas

Then I'll share a quick round of rough directions.

3. We'll decide on a direction

Reviewing the rough concepts and overall visual goal.

4. I'll present the final logo

It will be awesome and you will love it.

5. You'll give a thumbs-up

Or, I'll make one round of revisions.

6. I'll deliver your assets

Everything you need for web and print.
