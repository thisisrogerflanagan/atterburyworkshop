Atterbury is a tiny workshop, type foundry, and goods shop created by accidental-entrepreneur and type tinkerer, Roger Flanagan. We make letters and numbers (and goods with those letters and numbers on them) for people like you. Handmade in Northern Kentucky.

We create unique-but-familiar display typefaces that'll help designers make interesting and fun things. Often inspired by vintage lettering and type, our alphabets and goods are genuine and shipped worldwide.

Always Casual. Never Proper.

SimpleBits has also been a design studio and blog for over two decades. We are, at times, available for logo design projects.
